Introduction

NIMFA operates this store and website, including all related information, content, features, tools, products, and services, to provide you, the customer, with a carefully curated shopping experience (the “Services”).

NIMFA is powered by the Shopify platform, which enables us to provide the Services to you. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you visit, use, make a purchase, or otherwise interact with us through the Services. If there is a conflict between our Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy, this Privacy Policy will govern regarding the collection, processing, and disclosure of your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read this policy and understand how your data is collected, used, and disclosed as described.

About us

NIMFA is an online store specializing in sexual wellness products, operating in the Netherlands and hosted on the Shopify platform. We act as the data controller for all personal data collected via:
• our website (www.nimfa.nl),
• customer service forms,
• newsletter subscriptions,
• and purchases.

You can contact us at: nimfashopnl@gmail.com or Kwadijk 168, 1471 CK, Kwadijk, Netherlands.

Personal data we collect

• Contact information: name, email, phone number, billing and shipping address
• Account details: email, password, preferences
• Purchase data: orders, cart items, payment method (processed via Shopify/Stripe)
• Marketing preferences: newsletter subscriptions
• Device and usage information: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via cookies)
• Communication history: emails, support messages

Legal basis for processing (GDPR Art. 6)

• Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) – for newsletters and marketing emails
• Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) – to fulfill orders
• Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) – e.g., tax record retention
• Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) – fraud prevention, website analytics

How we use your data

• To process and deliver your orders
• To manage your account and preferences
• To send transactional and marketing communications
• To provide customer support
• To comply with legal obligations
• To improve our services and website

Sharing your data

We only share your personal data when necessary:
• Shopify – store platform and hosting
• Klaviyo – for email marketing and automation
• Sendcloud – for shipping and tracking
• Payment processors – Shopify Payments, iDEAL, credit cards
• Customer service providers – Google Forms, Gmail
• Authorities – only if legally required

All partners are GDPR-compliant and provide adequate protection.

Security measures

• SSL encryption (HTTPS)
• Secure payment gateways (via Shopify)
• Limited access to data (authorized personnel only)

However, no online system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users immediately.

Data retention

We retain your data:
• as long as your account is active,
• as required for legal or tax purposes,
• or until you request deletion.

Your rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at nimfashopnl@gmail.com.
