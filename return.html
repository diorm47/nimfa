Return and Refund Policy

General provisions

At NIMFA, we value the trust of our customers. Our products fall under the category of intimate and hygienic items, which means returns or exchanges are only possible under specific conditions in accordance with EU and Dutch law.

This policy has been established in accordance with Articles 6:230p and 6:230s of the Dutch Civil Code, as well as the EU Consumer Rights Directive.

Exceptions to the right of withdrawal

According to the law, you do not have the right to withdraw your order within 14 days if:
• the product is of an intimate or hygienic nature;
• the seal has been broken or the packaging has been opened;
• the product shows signs of use or has come into contact with the body.

These rules are designed to ensure the health, safety, and privacy of all our customers.

When returns are possible

Returns are only accepted in the following cases:
• manufacturing defect;
• damage during transport;
• incorrect product delivered (model, color, quantity).

Conditions:
• return requests must be submitted within 14 days of delivery;
• the product is unused, unopened, and in its original condition.

How to request a return

Steps to submit a return request:
 1. Send an email to nimfashopnl@gmail.com
 2. Include:
• your order number;
• a clear description of the issue;
• clear photos or videos of the damage or error.

We will respond within 3 business days with further instructions.

Return costs

• If the error is not on the part of NIMFA, the return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.
• If the error is ours, all costs will be fully covered by us.

Refunds

After receiving and inspecting the product:
• you will receive a confirmation email;
• the amount will be refunded within 7–14 business days via the original payment method;
• shipping costs are non-refundable unless the error is ours.
