Shipping Policy

Delivery area

Delivery is available throughout the Netherlands via reliable courier services, including PostNL, DHL, and other partners.

Delivery time

The delivery time is 1–7 business days from the confirmation of payment. In case of delays caused by the carrier, we are not liable, but we are always happy to assist in resolving any issues.

Shipping costs

Shipping costs are automatically calculated at checkout, depending on the courier service and delivery address.
All orders are subject to shipping costs, regardless of the order amount.

Order processing

Orders are processed within 24–48 hours after payment confirmation on business days. In case of force majeure or technical issues, the customer will be notified by email.

Packaging

All products are packed in discreet, opaque packaging without any indication of the contents. This ensures the privacy and comfort of our customers.

Delivery confirmation

A delivery is considered successful based on the carrier’s tracking information showing the status “delivered.”
A signature upon delivery is not required to speed up the process.
If the tracking indicates successful delivery but the customer reports not having received the package, the company reserves the right to refuse compensation unless there is clear evidence of loss or a carrier error.

Failed deliveries

If delivery fails due to customer error (incorrect address, absence), reshipping costs will be borne by the customer.
Undelivered packages will be returned within 7–14 days. We will then contact you to arrange reshipment or a refund (excluding shipping costs).

Order tracking

After shipping, the customer will receive a tracking number by email to follow the delivery independently on the carrier’s website.

Change of delivery address

Changes to the delivery address are only possible before shipment. After the package has been handed over to the carrier, no further address changes can be made.
